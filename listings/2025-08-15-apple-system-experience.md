# Software Engineer, System Experience - Apple

Source: https://jobs.apple.com/en-us/details/200602358/software-engineer-system-experience  
Role number: 200602358  
Posted: May 01, 2025  
Location: Cupertino, California, United States

## Summary

The System Experience team is looking for a proactive and highly skilled engineer to build new experiences and future technologies. Come work with a creative and hardworking team of engineers, multi-functional teams, and world-class designers to deliver the best experience on the planet!

## Description

As an engineer on the System Experience team, your responsibilities will range from implementing new features and defining API’s to fixing bugs and improving performance. You should have experience developing user interfaces, an excellent understanding of object-oriented software design, good debugging skills and an eagerness to work hard and learn a lot. As our team works cross-functionally with many other groups across our software stack, you should have a good understanding of systems and excellent communication and collaboration skills!

## Minimum Qualifications

- Excellent Swift programming and design skills
- Exceptional problem solving, critical thinking, and communication skills
- Inter-process communication and systems development experience
- Deep understanding of optimization and performance issues across OS software layers

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience developing reusable API’s and frameworks
