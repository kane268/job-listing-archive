About the team
Stripe’s Experimentation Platform sits at the heart of how we build
products. The platform provides random assignment, metrics
computation, rigorous statistical analysis, versatile reports that let
every Stripe team ship confidently, learn quickly, and keep users
safe. We serve experiments at scale, powering decisions for
launches that move trillions of dollars of payments volume. The team
is a tight-knit group of engineers who partner closely with data
scientists and product thinkers, united by a commitment to
correctness, low latency, and developer ergonomics.
What you’ll do
We’re looking for a Staff Software Engineer who will set the technical
direction for the next generation of Stripe’s Experimentation
Platform—one that is as intuitive for internal users to adopt as it is
powerful under the hood. You will architect and build systems that
provide easy setup, low-latency results, and statistically sound
analysis, all while being simple to integrate, flexible to extend, and
ready to scale an order of magnitude beyond today’s traffic. You will
partner closely with product teams and data scientists to ensure
they have a seamless end-to-end experimentation experience—
from defining a hypothesis to making a launch decision. Your work
will directly influence how quickly and safely Stripe ships every new
feature.
Responsibilities
Define and drive the long-term architecture of Stripe’s
Experimentation Platform.

Who you are
We’re looking for someone who meets the minimum requirements to
be considered for the role. If you meet these requirements, you are
encouraged to apply. The preferred qualifications are a bonus, not a
requirement.
Minimum requirements
Lead multi-quarter, cross-org initiatives that improve reliability,
scalability, and statistical rigor of the platform.
Directly contribute to core systems and write code.
Ensure operational excellence and enable a highly available,
reliable and secure Experimentation Platform
Partner with engineers and data scientists across the company
on experimentation best practices and build a community with
an metrics-first & experimentation-first culture.
10+ years of professional software engineering experience, with
3+ years acting as a technical lead for large-scale distributed
systems.
Strong understanding of statistical experimentation concepts
(A/B testing, power analysis, confidence intervals, multiple-
testing corrections)
Proficient in at least one of the modern programming languages
(e.g. Java, Ruby, Python, Typescript).
Hands-on experience with stream or batch data processing
technologies (Kafka, Flink, Spark, Trino, or equivalent)
Demonstrated ability to partner with data scientists, product
managers, and other engineers to translate business questions
into reliable technical solutions

Preferred qualifications
Office-assigned Stripes spend at least 50% of the time in a
given month in their local office or with users. This hits a
balance between bringing people together for in-person

The annual US base salary range for this role is $224,000 -
$336,000. For sales roles, the range provided is the role’s On
Target Earnings ("OTE") range, meaning that the range
